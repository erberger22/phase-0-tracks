Using the Elevator
-Locate the bottom most button in the elevator
	-ask “is your room on floor {symbol on button}?”
		-if responsive is “no” move up to the next button
		-if response is “yes” press current button

Folding a towel
-pick up towel in both hands
-move both hands along towel together until they reach an edge (side a)
-move hands away from each other 
	-continue to move each hand until it reaches the end of side a (each hand on a corner)
		-bring corners together
		-hold corners together with right hand and move left hand along vertical edge until it reaches a corner
		-bring corners together
